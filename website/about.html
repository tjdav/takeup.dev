Master programming fundamentals and unlock your potential in web development.

Whether you're starting your coding journey, refining your skills, transitioning into web development, or aiming to excel in your current role, Take-up Dev is your gateway to mastering programming fundamentals and thriving in the tech industry.

What is Take-up Dev?

Take-up Dev is a private online academy dedicated to helping learners master the core principles of web development. Our philosophy is simple:

Libraries and frameworks come and go, but programming fundamentals are forever.

Our mission is to build your confidence in coding by solidifying the fundamentals. We focus on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the cornerstone technologies of the web. These skills not only enable you to create functional, interactive, and visually appealing websites, but also lay the groundwork for exploring advanced tools like React, Vue.js, and back-end technologies. Through our hands-on approach, we ensure that you develop sustainable, transferable skills that will help you navigate the ever-changing tech landscape with assurance and adaptability.

What will you learn at Take-up Dev?

At Take-up Dev, you’ll build a solid foundation in web development by mastering programming fundamentals:

  • HTML: Learn to structure web pages effectively with semantic HTML, ensuring accessibility, user-friendliness, and alignment with industry standards.
  • CSS: Transform your designs into visually stunning websites. Master responsive layouts, create captivating animations, and implement user-engaging techniques.
  • JavaScript: Gain a deep understanding of JavaScript, the backbone of web interactivity. Build dynamic, engaging user experiences without relying on external libraries.
  • Typescript: Learn to catch errors before runtime, implement interfaces and generics, and use advanced type systems to build more robust applications.
  • Accessibility: Build inclusive websites that everyone can use, including individuals with disabilities, while adhering to global accessibility standards.

Our adaptable curriculum is designed to accommodate the diverse needs of each learner. Rather than following a rigid program, we take an individualized approach, guiding you toward mastery of the fundamentals while setting achievable weekly goals to ensure consistent progress. Along the way, you’ll also develop best practices, problem-solving skills, analytical thinking, and the autonomy needed to take on advanced challenges and thrive as a developer.

Who is Take-up Dev for?

Take-up Dev is for anyone passionate about learning programming, regardless of background.

  • Complete beginners: Never written a line of code? No worries! Our step-by-step approach breaks down complex concepts into clear, actionable lessons. Whether you're exploring coding out of curiosity or dreaming of building your own websites, we’ll guide you patiently, making the learning process enjoyable and accessible.
  • Students and self-learners: Whether you're in school or teaching yourself to code, our personalized mentorship complements your studies, helping to bridge any gaps in your understanding of the fundamentals. With a strong foundation, you'll accelerate your progress and gain greater independence in your learning.
  • Job seekers and professionals: Ready to launch a career in web development, advance in your current role or develop a business as a freelancer? We’re here to help you. Our practical, hands-on guidance will give you the confidence and expertise needed to stand out in the competitive tech industry and reach your professional or entrepreneurial goals.
